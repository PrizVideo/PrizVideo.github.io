What is PrizVideo?

PrizVideo is a video broadcasting service, similar to YouTube, but it doesn't have advertisements and is privacy based. What's the twist? There isn't one. Did I mention it is open source?


Please note that PrizVideo is still a work in progress. It is also currently in limbo, as many things needed to continue development are not currently available. A time for when development will be resumed is unknown. We are trying our hardest, though!
